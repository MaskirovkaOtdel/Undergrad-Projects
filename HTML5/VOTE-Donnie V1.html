Important Election Information

Election Day: November 5, 2024

Polls Open: 7:00 AM

Polls Close: 8:00 PM (Local Time)

Voter Eligibility

  • U.S. Citizen
  • At least 18 years old on Election Day
  • Resident of your state for at least 30 days before the election
  • Not disqualified by felony conviction (check with your state for specific laws)

How to Vote

  1. Register to Vote:

    Deadline to register varies by state, typically 15-30 days before Election Day. Register to Vote Online

  2. Early Voting:

    Some states allow early voting. Find out if it's available in your area! Check Early Voting Locations

  3. Vote by Mail:

    Request a mail-in ballot if your state allows it. Be sure to request it early. Request a Mail-In Ballot

  4. On Election Day:

    Go to your polling place. Make sure to bring a valid ID. If you're in line before polls close, you will still be allowed to vote.
