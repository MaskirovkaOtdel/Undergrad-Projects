Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 100g Pancetta
  • 2 large Eggs
  • 50g Parmesan Cheese
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Instructions:

Cook the spaghetti in salted water. In a pan, cook pancetta until crispy. Beat the eggs and mix with Parmesan. Combine pasta, pancetta, and egg mixture. Serve immediately.

Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • 500g Chicken breast
  • 1 Onion, chopped
  • 2 Garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp Curry Powder
  • 200ml Coconut Milk

Instructions:

Saute onions and garlic. Add chicken, curry powder, and coconut milk. Simmer until the chicken is cooked through. Serve with rice or bread.
