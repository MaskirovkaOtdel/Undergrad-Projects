KIM JONG UN

Glory to Supreme Leader and Secretary

In the land where the mountains rise high,
A leader stands with an unblinking eye.
Kim Jong Un, with power in hand,
Guides his people through a troubled land.

Through corridors of silence, he walks alone,
A figure whose strength is keenly shown.
A symbol of might, both feared and revered,
His rule is one that many have feared.

With nuclear dreams and a hardened gaze,
He navigates through the world's complex maze.
Tensions rise, as the world looks on,
As he holds the reins, his will goes on.

But behind the throne, a nation waits,
Hoping for change, as it contemplates.
In the shadow of power, the people pray,
For peace to come, in a brighter day.
